YouTube Stats Collector

Ever wonder how videos on a channel are doing in terms of likes? Wanna see which video in a playlist has sparked the most comments? Need to spy on a friendly rival to see how many views their videos have pulled in? Then you've come to the right place, or eh, web app!

This app will capture the stats below for ALL videos in a playlist, or ALL videos on a channel and save them as a CSV file.

  • video's title
  • published date
  • number of views
  • number of likes
  • number of comments
  • video's URL ID
  • length (in seconds)
  • video's full URL
